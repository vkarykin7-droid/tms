# tms
Build your career in the world of football transfers
Transfer Market Simulator is a realistic football market simulator where every player chooses their own path and influences the fates of clubs, players, and entire leagues.

There is no single way to achieve success in the game. You decide who you want to become in the world of professional football.

⚽ Role 1. Player Agent
Become an influential football agent and manage the careers of your clients.

Your tasks:

Negotiate between clubs.

Persuade teams to buy your players.

Secure the best contracts and salaries.

Resolve conflicts between the player and the club.

Enhance your agency's reputation and close high-profile deals.

Note: Every decision affects the footballers' careers and your reputation in the market.

🔍 Role 2. Scout
Discover future stars before anyone else.

Your tasks:

Search for promising players worldwide.

Analyze stats, potential, and playstyles.

Compile reports for clubs.

Compete with other scouts for the most talented finds.

Note: The clubs' transfer policies heavily depend on your recommendations.

🏆 Role 3. Club President
Take charge of a football club and make crucial decisions.

Your tasks:

Control the transfer budget.

Buy and sell players.

Work with the scouting department.

Negotiate with agents.

Develop the club and fight for trophies.

Note: Every transfer can either be a brilliant move or a costly mistake.

🎯 Role 4. Head Coach
Build your dream team.

Your tasks:

Identify weak positions in the squad.

Look for the right players to fit your tactics.

Communicate with scouts and study their reports.

Convince the club president to allocate funds for transfers.

Negotiate with agents through the club's management.

Note: The team's success depends not only on tactics but also on your ability to influence the club's transfer policy.

A Living Football Market
In the game world, all parties are interconnected:

The Agent offers players.

The Scout searches for talents.

The Coach creates requests to strengthen the squad.

The President makes the final decision.

Every participant pursues their own interests, turning every deal into a true strategic game and a negotiation battle.

Main Feature
The player independently chooses who to play as:

⚽ Player Agent

🔍 Scout

🏆 Club President

🎯 Head Coach

Each role offers a unique gameplay experience, its own goals, a progression system, and a completely different perspective on the world of football transfers.
